Security protocols for absolute confidentiality

Security Compliance Requirements

By accessing PsyGen, you agree to comply with our comprehensive security protocols designed to maintain absolute confidentiality and data protection.

Screenshot & Recording Restrictions

Users are strictly prohibited from attempting to capture, record, or document any content from the platform. Our advanced detection systems monitor and prevent screenshot attempts, screen recordings, and any form of visual data capture. Violation of these restrictions will result in immediate session termination.

Tab Switching & Multi-Tasking Monitoring

The platform employs real-time monitoring systems that detect tab switching, window changes, and multi-application usage during active sessions. Any attempt to navigate away from the platform or engage in concurrent activities will trigger security protocols and may result in session suspension.

Copy-Paste & Data Extraction Prevention

All text selection, copy operations, right-click functions, and keyboard shortcuts for data extraction are systematically disabled. Users cannot copy, paste, or extract any content from the platform. These measures ensure complete data containment and prevent unauthorized information transfer.

Screen Sharing & Remote Access Detection

Our security infrastructure actively detects screen sharing applications (including Google Meet, Zoom, Teams, and other platforms), remote desktop connections, and collaborative viewing tools. Any detection of such activities will immediately terminate the session to maintain confidentiality.

Behavioral Monitoring & Malpractice Detection

The platform continuously monitors user behavior patterns to identify potential security breaches, unauthorized access attempts, or circumvention of security measures. Suspicious activities are automatically flagged and may result in permanent access restriction.

1:1 Confidentiality Measures

PsyGen maintains strict one-to-one confidentiality protocols ensuring that each user session is completely isolated and private. No data sharing, cross-referencing, or collaborative access is permitted under any circumstances.

Acceptable Use Policy

Users must access the platform individually and privately. Group usage, shared access, or collaborative sessions are strictly prohibited. The platform is designed for individual psychological assessment and analysis only.

Violation Consequences

Any attempt to bypass security measures, violate confidentiality protocols, or engage in prohibited activities will result in immediate session termination and potential permanent access restriction.

Agreement Acknowledgment

By using PsyGen, you acknowledge understanding and agreement to these terms and commit to maintaining the highest standards of security and confidentiality.
