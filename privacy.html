Privacy Policy

Your data security is our absolute priority

Data Localization & Zero Storage

PsyGen operates on a strict zero-data-retention policy. All psychological analyses, personality assessments, and mental health evaluations are processed in real-time without any data storage, logging, or retention. Your personal information, responses, and analysis results are never saved, cached, or transmitted to third-party servers.

Complete Data Isolation

Our platform ensures 100% data localization through client-side processing and ephemeral server interactions. No user data, conversation history, or analytical results are stored in databases, temporary files, or backup systems. Each session operates independently with complete data isolation.

Advanced Security Measures

Screenshot & Screen Recording Protection

Our platform implements advanced browser-level security protocols that disable screenshot capabilities, screen recording functions, and print screen operations. These measures prevent unauthorized data capture and ensure complete session confidentiality.

Copy-Paste Restrictions

All text selection, copy operations, and paste functions are systematically disabled across the platform. This prevents data extraction through clipboard operations and maintains the integrity of your private information.

Third-Party Attack Prevention

We employ comprehensive security frameworks including Content Security Policy (CSP), Cross-Origin Resource Sharing (CORS) restrictions, and real-time threat detection systems. These measures protect against data interception, man-in-the-middle attacks, and unauthorized access attempts.

Session Security

Each user session operates within an isolated environment with encrypted data transmission. Session tokens are ephemeral and automatically expire upon browser closure. No persistent cookies or tracking mechanisms are employed.

AI Processing Transparency

All AI analyses are performed through secure API endpoints with encrypted data transmission. Processing occurs in isolated computational environments without data persistence or cross-session contamination.

Compliance & Standards

Our privacy practices exceed industry standards for psychological data protection and comply with international data protection regulations. We maintain the highest levels of confidentiality equivalent to clinical psychological practice standards.
